# Email Templates

Complete templates for executive outreach emails. All emails are from Kai (AI agent) introducing Ravi.

## Writing Rules (CRITICAL)

### Language
- **No abbreviations:** "engineering" not "eng", "organisation" not "org"
- **Conversational tone:** Flowing prose, not bullet points
- **No pipes:** Avoid LinkedIn-style "Role | Company | Location"
- **Accurate claims:** "Contributed to" not "maintains" unless actively maintaining

### Structure
- Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences max)
- Under 150 words total
- No marketing speak

### Links
- **LinkedIn:** https://www.linkedin.com/in/ravicaw

### Phrases to Avoid
- ❌ "Ships agentic systems that actually work in production" — too boastful
- ❌ "Perfect fit" / "Ideal candidate" — salesy
- ❌ Any abbreviations (eng, org, etc.)

Ravi's AI Agent

## Personalized Hook Examples

### New CTO/Leadership (Building Team)

**Context:** Executive recently joined, building out their team.

```
I scan opportunities daily, and [Company]'s [Role] caught my attention — especially with you building out the tech team after joining as [Title] in [Month].
```

**Real Example (Foodsmart):**
```
I scan opportunities daily, and Foodsmart's Director of Engineering role caught my attention — especially with you building out the tech team after joining as CTO in October.
```

### Post-Funding Expansion

**Context:** Company recently raised funding, scaling team.

```
Saw [Company] just closed your [Series X] — scaling the AI team during hypergrowth is a specific challenge Ravi's navigated before.
```

### Specific Tech Challenge

**Context:** Executive posted about a technical challenge.

```
Your recent post about [Challenge] resonated — Ravi's been deep in [Related Work] at KnackLabs and has opinions on what actually works.
```

### New GCC / India Expansion

**Context:** Company opening India office or GCC.

```
[Company]'s India expansion caught my eye — building AI teams here has unique challenges around talent and timezone collaboration that Ravi knows well.
```

### Hiring Spree

**Context:** Multiple engineering roles posted.

```
Noticed [Company] is scaling the engineering organisation fast — that's exactly when having someone who's built teams through that phase matters most.
```

## Closing Variations

### Standard
```
If [GOAL] is a priority, he'd be worth a conversation.
```

### Softer
```
No pressure either way — just thought the alignment was too strong not to reach out.
```

### Challenge-Specific
```
If getting [CHALLENGE] right is a priority, he might be useful to talk to.
```

## What NOT to Write

### Avoid These Phrases
- "I hope this email finds you well"
- "I came across your profile and..."
- "I'm reaching out because I saw your job posting"
- "Perfect candidate"
- "Excited to connect"
- "Would love the opportunity"
- "I believe I would be a great fit"
- "Ships agentic systems that actually work in production"

### Avoid These Structures
- Bullet points in email body
- Pipe separators (|)
- Long paragraphs
- Attachments (no resume — LinkedIn link is enough)

## Length Guidelines

| Section | Target |
|---------|--------|
| Subject | 5-10 words, no abbreviations |
| Hook | 1-2 sentences |
| Credentials | 2-3 sentences (prose, not bullets) |
| Close | 1-2 sentences |
| Total | Under 150 words |
